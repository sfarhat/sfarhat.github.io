About Me

I'm Sean, an undergraduate student at the University of California, Berkeley, studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. This past summer, I interned at Accenture Labs doing research on their Systems and Platform Team, focusing on safe robotic applications in factory settings. I have also done research academically in Laurent El Ghaoui's group on robust optimization and implicit deep learning, and the SWARM Lab, where I worked on autonomously driving microrobots. This upcoming semester, I will be teaching EECS 127: Optimization Models, while in the past I have taught CS 61C: Machine Structures.
